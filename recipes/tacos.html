Tacos

Description

This is a recipe for taco. Tacos originate from Mexico. There are hundreds of variants of tacos. This one is a simple variation that involves mince and a packets of spice mix.

Ingredients

  • Woolworths spice mix
  • 500 g mince
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Sliced cheese
  • Spinach wraps

Steps

  1. Add the mince and a splash of oil to a wok on high heat
  2. Once the meat is browned, add the spice mix along with 1/2 a cup of water
  3. Slice the lettuce and dice the tomato
  4. Add the mince hot, along with the cheese, lettuce and tomato to the wrap and serve
