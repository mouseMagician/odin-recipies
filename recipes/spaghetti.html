Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe for Spaghetti. Spaghetti is a meal that originates from Italy. It consists of a crushed tomato sauce that has been infused with oregano, garlic, onion and basil. This is served with long stranded pasta, traditionally made fresh. Many variations of spaghetti exist, such as with mince or meatballs.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of napoletana sauce
  • 500 g mince
  • A handfull of pasta
  • Whole Onion

Steps

  1. Add diced onion to pan on medium heat with a splash of oil
  2. Simultaneously, add the pasta to a pot, 3/4 of the way filled with water
  3. Brown the mince
  4. Add the sauce
  5. Serve hot
